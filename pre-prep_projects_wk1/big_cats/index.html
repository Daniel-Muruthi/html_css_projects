Lions

Here are some fun facts about Lions.

  • A lion can weigh up to 250kgs
  • A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 kilometres away.
  • Female lions hunt, while male lions guard their territory.

What to do when you bump into a Lion:

  1. Freeze
  2. Don't make eye contact
  3. Make as much noise as you can to scare it.

Homo Sapiens

Here are some fun facts about homo sapiens.

  • Homo sapiens are the only animals known to have landed on the moon.
  • Homo sapiens are the most intelligent animals on the planet earth
  • The average lifespan of a homo sapien is 100 years.

The different types of Homo Sapiens existing today include:

  1. African
  2. Europeans
  3. Asian
  4. Arabs
  5. Semites
  6. Native Americans
  7. Oceanians
