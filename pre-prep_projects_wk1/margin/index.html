Udacity

Acording to Wikipedia, Udacity is "Udacity is a for-profit educational organization founded by Sebastian Thrun, David Stavens, and Mike Sololsky offering massive open online courses (MOOCs)."

put simply, udacity is an online learning platform offering free or cheap access to quality education in computer science. It is a resource that I encourage anyone interested in software technology to check out. If you like SPOC, Udacity is is for you!
